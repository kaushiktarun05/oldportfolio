Intro

Welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Tarun Kaushik, and I am a second-year student pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering with a specialization in Cyber Security at the prestigious National Forensic Sciences University. As a passionate and curious learner, I have always been fascinated by the intersection of technology and security, and my academic journey has been driven by a desire to explore and contribute to this exciting field. Throughout my coursework, I have gained a solid foundation in the fundamental concepts of computer science, including programming, algorithms, and data structures. Additionally, my specialization in cyber security has provided me with a comprehensive understanding of the threats and vulnerabilities that modern computer systems face, as well as the tools and techniques used to mitigate them. As I continue my academic journey and work towards my degree, I am eager to gain hands-on experience through internships and other opportunities. I believe that my passion for cyber security, coupled with my strong technical skills and academic background, make me a valuable asset to any organization seeking to protect their digital assets and infrastructure. Thank you for visiting my portfolio website, and I look forward to connecting with you soon! Do check out my awesome work.

Work

In addition to my academic coursework and internships, I have also participated in several programs and initiatives that have allowed me to gain practical experience and expand my skills in the field of cyber security. As part of my coursework and extracurricular activities, I have completed several projects and programs. For example, I was a fellow of INSIG 2022, held in Hyderabad, where I participated in workshops and discussions on the latest trends and challenges in cyber security. Additionally, I am a Google Cloud Facilitator Student, where I help students and professionals learn about cloud computing and security. I have also completed the Deloitte STEM Virtual Connect internship program, where I worked with digital forensics and cyber security. Also have participated in several conferences and hackathons, where I have learned from and collaborated with other cyber security enthusiasts. Through these experiences, I have gained a deeper understanding of the challenges and opportunities in this field, and I am eager to continue learning and contributing in any way I can.

If you are interested in working with me or have any questions about my experience, please do not hesitate to reach out.
